SCP 3128-B


Classification: Euclid


Description: SCP 3128-B stands at 1.82 metre and weighs approximately 77. Kilogram. It takes the form of a humanoid with a twisted torso and off proportion facial area.


Containment Procedure: SCP 3128-B is to be kept in an air tight glass cage with air being vented through eight 1mm holes in the ceiling protected by an electrified grate. SCP 3128-B must be kept under constant surveilance.


First Appearance: SCP 3128-B was first spotted in the [EXPUNGED] forest in East Ukraine.


Known Characteristics: SCP 3128-B has no solid skeleton and thus is able to squeeze into small spaces and through holes. SCP 3128-B is extremely hostile and is extremely strong and can rip through reinforced steel with it’s bare hands this makes it an extreme threat to personnel in the event of an escape.


Quirks: SCP 3128-B Possesses the ability to mimic human speech

currently it identifies itself under the name of “Darius”.
